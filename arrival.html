Getting Here

Arrival & Transportation

Airport, rideshare, train, and parking guidance for Venue SIX10.

ORD

O'Hare

Chicago's largest airport. Expect 35–60 minutes to downtown by rideshare depending on traffic.

MDW

Midway

Often the faster airport for downtown access. Expect 25–45 minutes by rideshare.

CTA

Train

Blue Line from O'Hare or Orange Line from Midway connect into the Loop.

CAR

Rideshare

Use Venue SIX10, 610 S Michigan Ave, as the destination for Uber, Lyft, or taxi.

Arrival Tip

Give yourself extra time downtown.

August traffic, hotel check-in, and downtown events can affect timing. Arriving near the venue early is the easiest way to keep the day smooth.
