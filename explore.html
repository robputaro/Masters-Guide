Curated Chicago Guide

Food, coffee, views, and classic Chicago experiences.

Everything below is intended to be easy from downtown Chicago — ideal for attendees arriving early, staying the weekend, or looking for a simple post-event plan.

Best low-effort plan: dinner near Millennium Park, a Riverwalk stroll, then a short rideshare back to your hotel.
Most Chicago option: deep dish, Millennium Park, and an architecture river cruise.
Planning note: August weekends are busy. Book restaurants and tours early.
