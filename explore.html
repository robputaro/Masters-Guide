Chicago Weekend Guide

Keep it easy, classic, and close to downtown.

These ideas are meant for attendees who want a simple Chicago experience without over-planning the weekend.

Best low-effort plan: dinner downtown, Riverwalk, then a short rideshare back to your hotel.
Most Chicago option: deep dish, Millennium Park, and an architecture river cruise.
Planning note: August weekends are busy. Book restaurants and tours early.
